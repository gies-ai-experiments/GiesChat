Tradeoffs & open questions

Should MCP, Skills & Plugins really share one “Tools” home?

This assumes they’re similar enough to co-locate as tabs. If the team sees them as distinct products, keep them separate — but at minimum lift Plugins out of the hidden “⋯” menu onto the rail, so tools aren’t split between visible and buried.

Icon rail vs. a full labeled sidebar?

I kept the compact icon rail to preserve the chat’s horizontal space; group labels show when expanded and on hover. If discoverability for a non-technical audience matters more than width, an expandable labeled rail (labels persistent) is the safer default.

Migration order

Low-risk sequence: (1) rename Brainstorm → Rooms and standardize panel headers; (2) group the rail with dividers/labels; (3) consolidate Files and Conversations; (4) merge Agents and Tools (the larger IA change). Each step ships independently.
