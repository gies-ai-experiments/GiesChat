\maketitle

\begin{abstract}
[150--250 words. Problem and why it matters; the gap; what you built; the key measured
result with a concrete number; the takeaway. No citations. Write this last.]
\end{abstract}

\begin{IEEEkeywords}
[keyword1, keyword2, keyword3, keyword4]
\end{IEEEkeywords}

\section{Introduction}\label{sec:intro}
[Funnel: domain and why it's hard $\rightarrow$ shortfall in current practice $\rightarrow$
your approach in 2--3 sentences $\rightarrow$ explicit contribution list $\rightarrow$ paper roadmap.]

The contributions of this paper are:
\begin{itemize}
  \item [Contribution 1]
  \item [Contribution 2]
  \item [Contribution 3]
\end{itemize}

\section{Related Work}\label{sec:related}
[Thematic groups of prior art; for each, how this work differs. Real, verifiable
citations only, e.g.~\cite{ref1}. Include needed background.]

\section{Methodology}\label{sec:method}
[Architecture overview (lead with Fig.~\ref{fig:arch}); each component's job, I/O, and
algorithm; key design decisions and \emph{why}; equations/pseudocode where they help.]

\section{Implementation}\label{sec:impl}
[Languages/frameworks, notable libraries, scale, engineering challenges, determinism/
safety mechanisms. Optional---merge into Methodology if thin.]

\section{Evaluation}\label{sec:eval}
[Experimental setup (environment, config, data, seeds, sample sizes); metric definitions;
results in tables/figures with interpretation. REAL numbers from evidence/ only. State
plainly anything that could not be run.]

\section{Discussion and Limitations}\label{sec:discussion}
[Higher-level interpretation; candid threats to validity (internal, external, construct);
where the approach would not apply.]

\section{Conclusion}\label{sec:conclusion}
[Restate contribution and strongest evidence (no new claims); concrete future work.]
